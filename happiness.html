Happiness

Happiness is an emotional state characterized by feelings of joy, satisfaction, contentment, and fulfillment.

While happiness has many different definitions, it is often described as involving positive emotions and life satisfaction.

Self-Satisfaction

Life is a blessing and it is a world which is full of reality. It is natural that every human on the earth wants to know the reason behind things happening to them. Say for example everyone wants to know about the reason for his birth in this world, daily activities which are carried out by him, reason for education or many other activities of the routine. Usually, humans remain worried by thinking about such daily happenings in their life and sometimes thinking about the things which may not even happen.
Problems are just a part of everyone’s life. The God who created problem will also provide a solution to the problem. But people keep thinking about the problems they have in life. Every men and women wants things to happen as they wish and when things take a deviation from their wish, it gives them stress. They keep cursing God for making such a mess and complain that things are wrong with them only. It is difficult to lead a happy and peaceful life if one keeps blaming and sticking to the problems alone in life. Problems may appear and vanish but Life is once. Failing to live a happy life once missed is missed .It cannot be gained back. To lead a happy and healthy life Self Satisfaction plays a key and vital role. Self-satisfaction should be achieved in all aspects of life. Achieving self-satisfaction lies within oneself. Balancing physical and emotional issues can lead us to achieve self-satisfaction. Self-satisfaction is the feeling of being content. It is when people around us are high and keep growing stop being jealous and worried. Be happy and contended with what you have.

Ways to achieve self-satisfaction:-

  • Positive Thinking Process:- Though you don’t attain any growth or good things in life don’t envy at the ones who attain growth and popularity.
  • Stress free mind:- Do not ever have any sort of negativity in your mind. Negativity is a strong weapon that destroys one’s life. Whenever you feel negative stay focused to achieve your goal and divert yourself to forget the negativity.
  • Begin your day with Happiness:- Happiness is one of the key elements to be self-satisfied. Start your day with full energy and happiness. The energy of your happiness may even cast your worries aside and boom your growth.
  • End your day with Gratitude:- Always be thankful at the end of the day whether it was good or bad. Things change and if it was even a bad day you may have a change and good days later.
  • Live the moment:- Never ever think about past and waste your time. Live each and every moment with full of energy. Thinking about past will not change anything. It changes only our mind and makes us overburdened.
  • Fix your Goals:- There is no necessity that goals should always be very high. Fix your goals according to your life. Try to achieve a small part of your goal at the end of every day. Achieving a nano size improvement also makes us feel much happy.
  • Have Positive people around:- 1 true friend is better than 100 fake friends around. It is very important to have good and positive people around. The person who is around us should be a booster of our energy. They should not be the one who ruins our positivity and thoughts.
  • Learn from mistakes:- When you commit a mistake once, all you must do is try to sort a way to clear the mistakes. When you keep repeating the same mistake again and again then you lose self-confidence. Once self-confidence is lost automatically we lose self-satisfaction.
  • Have time for yourself:- Since it is self-satisfaction you must find time for your own self. Take time to think the things you have done, things you are doing, things you will do. Try to find whether things are aligned right. Find out if there is any hindrance in your path. If so try to find the reason behind it and sort it out.
  • Don’t let your self-respect down:- Whether we are respected among others or not, we must try to respect ourselves. Respect oneself in all the ways. i.e respect your thoughts, respect your feelings, respect your opinions.

Be Happy

  1. Be with others who make you smile.

    Studies show that we are happiest when we are around those who are also happy. Stick with those who are joyful and let rub off on you.

  2. Hold on to your values.

    What you find true, what you know is fair, and what you believe in are all values. Over time, the more you honor them, the better you will feel about yourself and those you love.

  3. Accept the good.

    Look at your life and take stock of what’s working, and don’t push away something just because it isn’t perfect. When good things happen, even the very little ones, let them in.

  4. Imagine the best.

    Don’t be afraid to look at what you really want and see yourself getting it. Many people avoid this process because they don’t want to be disappointed if things don’t work out. The truth is that imagining getting what you want is a big part of achieving it.

  5. Do things you love.

    Maybe you can’t skydive every day or take vacations every season, but as long as you get to do the things you love every once in a while, you will find greater happiness.

  6. Find purpose.

    Those who believe they are contributing to the well-being of humanity tend to feel better about their lives. Most people want to be part of something greater than they are, simply because it’s fulfilling.

  7. Listen to your heart.

    You are the only one who knows what fills you up. Your family and friends may think you’d be great at something that really doesn’t float your boat. It can be complicated following your bliss. Just be smart, and keep your day job for the time being.

  8. Push yourself, not others.

    It’s easy to feel that someone else is responsible for your fulfillment, but the reality is that it is really your charge. Once you realize that, you have the power to get where you want to go. Stop blaming others or the world, and you’ll find your answers much sooner.

  9. Be open to change.

    Even if it doesn’t feel good, change is the one thing you can count on. Change will happen, so make contingency plans and emotionally shore yourself up for the experience.

  10. Bask in the simple pleasures.

    Those who love you, treasured memories, silly jokes, warm days, and starry nights—these are the ties that bind and the gifts that keep on giving.

    11. Happiness and fulfillment are within your grasp, but sometimes just out of reach. Understanding what works best for you is the first step in finding them more often.
